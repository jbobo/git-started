Rand Al'Thor

Rand Al'Thor is the protagonist of the Wheel of time. In a world where women with the ability to channel the Saidar rule the world, and men cursed with the ability are hunted down. Rand is the Dragon Reborn, the prophesied reincarnation of Lews Therin Kinslayer. Fated to one day defeat the Dark One in the Last Battle as well as destroy the world, he is both savior and villain to many. Doomed to madness by his ability to channel the tainted Power of Saidin. Rand must struggle to unite the world to survive the Last Battle, and the destruction he has been phrophesied to unleash.
